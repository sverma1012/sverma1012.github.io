Internships

Internships are an opportunity to gain experience in a formal and professional setting whilst being challenged to expand one's skills and abilities. They require a person to be comfortable with their skills and display them for the benefit of the institution and/or society.

Summer of 2016

Teresa Academy

I worked in Teresa Academy, a school associated with Maiti Nepal. I assisted the upper-kindergarten teacher in creating projects, taking care of and teaching the children. I handled tasks such as decorating the classroom, grading notebooks, teaching nursery rhymes, and teaching English to the students. As an assistant teacher, my main job was to create a safe environment for the students who either came from underprivileged households or were victims of human trafficking.

Summer of 2018

SAPNA Organization

I worked in the Trauma Centre and Safdarjung Hospital Dharamshala assisting patient outtakes and handling administrative tasks with my fellow interns. Further tasks that I conducted was distributing food to the sick and destitute in the Safdarjung Dharamshala and visiting patients in the Trauma Centre, along with providing technical and administrative assistance to the social workers.

Summer of 2018

English Tutor

Location: Residence in India. Children came from the colony behind my residence.

Description: As an Indian child from a middle-class income, I have lived in various places and have been surrounded by people with as much, more, or less opportunities than me. It was my social responsibility to spread the knowledge that I have gained from my education to others. I did this by teaching children from a slum in various subjects, such as English, Math, and Social Sciences at my house.

Summer of 2020

Economics Reseasrch Student

I worked as a research student for the Economics, Accounting and Management department under Dr. Samuel Bird. The grant was a competitve process in which the student receives a stipend. The reserach studied economic decision-making in the context of agriculture in sub-Saharan Africa.
