Incidental Externalities

Starting a WordPress Blog

WordPress is an open-source platform where people can create blogs or websites for free. It is easily accessible and available for people with or without experience in programming, thus, allowing for a larger range of audience.

Over the summer of 2019, I started a blog addressing various economical issues, such as economic fallacies, current economic issues etc. Through this blog, I hope to spread and increase economic awareness.

Being an economics student, my interests in the world's economic state has grown and widened. With current economic crises taking place between and within countries, it is important to tunderstand the basics of economics and its fallacies. In this blog I am writing about common economic pitfalls that should be avoided but often are not. Further, to create awareness I am writing about various economic crises that have taken place or arre taking place.
