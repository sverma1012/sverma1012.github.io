I Fell in Love With a Stranger

Writing a Fanfiction

A fanfiction is written by a writer using the characters from a particular piece of work, such as a book, tv series, movie etc.

I wrote a fanfiction based on the show Shadowhunters during my gap year from 2017-18. Through this project, I improved my creative writing and imaginative skills while also displaying these skills by publishing the work on a website.

After watching a television show or a movie, I have wanted to continue the story based on my feelings and interpretations. The urge to give my thoughts a tangible space and an audience larger than one has always existed and during my gap year, I decided to take action. After watching the television series, Shadowhunters I decided to write an alternate version with the same characters. I published each chapter on the website, Archive Of Our Own, which is a known website for publishing fanfictions.
