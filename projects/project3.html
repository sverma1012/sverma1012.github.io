Forgiveness Motivation

Filming and Editing Videos

A video is a medium that can used to convey messages, record people, etc. The process of publishing a video involves multiple steps beginning from scriptwriting and filming the video, followed by editing it to make it suitable for the desired audience.

As a project for my Psych 185 class, we were required to form groups and devise a method to motivate people to forgive and overcome grudges. My team and I decided to create two videos that would encourage people to follow the path of forgiveness.

During the course, I read and heard about the power of forgiveness in healing people mentally, emotionally, and physically. It was a powerful lesson that taught me the value of encouraging and invoking kindness and forgiveness. My team and I used YouTube, a digital platform, to create two videos to spread our knowledge and motivate people to follow the virtue of forgiveness to lead a peaceful and fulfilling life.

While my teammates shared in the task of filming the scenes, I edited them.
