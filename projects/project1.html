CODING FOR YOU

Building a Website

A website is a platform where a user can display various web resources for a multitude of reasons, ranging from academic reasons to personal ones.

I created a website during the senior year of my high school career as my senior project. My aim was to create a platform that can show that a girl with basic knowledge of web programming can be empowered to ccreate a powerful tool like a website.

Studying in Kathmandu, Nepal, I realized that women were uneducated in the latest technological developments as compared to most of their male counterparts. Upon contacting an NGO in Nepal, I learned that women were either not allowed to come near laptops and computers or did not deem themselves worthy enough to learn. Through my website I aimed to show that a young girl who was neither interested in web designing nor had any prior experience could make a website with basic training in the appropriate languages. After learning HTML and CSS through online courses over a period of 3 months, I successfully built a website to prove my point.
