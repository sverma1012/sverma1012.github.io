Sneha Verma

Maiti Nepal

Assistant Teacher

Location: Maiti Nepal is a non-profit organization that supports the victims of human trafficking in Nepal. It provides career and education opportunities for the victims. Teresa Academy is a school associated with Maiti Nepal.

I volunteered as an assistant teacher in the upper-kindergarten class. Through this experience, I gained the experience of interacting with people from a different culture, a different economic background, and with different experiences. Further, I developed my leadership and creativity skills.

Teresa Academy accepts underprivileged children and victims of human trafficking rescued by Maiti Nepal. Through the experience of working with these students, I developed skills and gained knowledge regarding culture, sensitivity, leadership, and sympathy.

As an assistant teacher, it was my responsibility to take care of the students in the absence of the teacher. I taught the students nursery rhymes in Hindi and English, and graded their tests and hiomework for all subjects. Further, I created various projects for the classroom, such as an interactive calendar, a drawing labelled with body parts, and a poster with pictures and names of animals, in an attempt to create an accepting and educational setting for the students.
