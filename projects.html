2018 - 2019

Creating videos on the importance of forgiveness

Description: Forgiveness is often a skill or quality taken for granted. However, as learned during a class during my first-year of college, the ability of forgiving does not come easily and can often be the cause of physical and mental health issues. Through this project, my teammates and I explored ways of motivating people to forgive and move on.

Result: Two YouTube videos briefly introducing people to the idea of forgiveness

Achievements: Worked on my video editing skills and spread the knowledge learned in college
