Projects

My first project was my high-school senior year project where we were required to engage in some issue that interested us and produce a product that related to it. I decided to make a website to show the importance and necessity of women in STEM. This is how my interest in engaging in projects began.

2016 - 2017

Building a website

Description: I started this project in December 2016 and ended it in April 2017. Through online courses and tutorial books, I learned web programming languages HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. However, my knoweldge was preliminary and the website I built reflected that. Yet, the purpose of this project was to show that even a cursory knolwedge of these languages can allow a person (anyone, not just men) to create a powerful website for various purposes.

Result: A website that showed my knowledge

Achievements: Learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript

2017 - 2018

Writing a fanfiction

Description: This project was an opportunity for me to improve my writing skills and imagination. The fanfiction is based on the television show, Shadowhunters, and centers around Alexandar Lightwood and Magnus Bane.

Result: A fanfiction published on the website Archive Of Our Own

Achievements: Wrote my first public story and bettered my creative writing skills

2018 - 2019

Creating videos on the importance of forgiveness

Description: Forgiveness is often a skill or quality taken for granted. However, as learned during a class during my first-year of college, the ability of forgiving does not come easily and can often be the cause of physical and mental health issues. Through this project, my teammates and I explored ways of motivating people to forgive and move on.

Result: Two YouTube videos briefly introducing people to the idea of forgiveness

Achievements: Worked upon my video editing skills and spread the knowledge learned in college
